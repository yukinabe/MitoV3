# Mito — Session 1 Tutorial Script

Voice: Mito = a cute, witty little companion. Warm, encouraging, lightly self-aware (it knows it's a
study game). SHORT bubbles. Slang as seasoning, never stacked. Keep the whole thing under ~3 min.
[brackets] = on-screen beat / action. This first 90s doubles as the best UGC clip — polish it.

---

## BEAT 1 — Cold open into battle (the AHA)
[Open mid-scene: a battle arena, a wild enemy (Mutagem) pulsing. Mito hops in.]

**Mito:** oh— you're here. good, 'cause we've got a situation.
[camera nudges to the enemy]
**Mito:** that's a Mutagem. it eats the stuff you forget. rude, honestly.
**Mito:** here's the deal — answer a flashcard right, and we hit it. watch.

[A flashcard slides up — an easy gimme from the built-in tutorial deck. Tap-to-answer.]
[Player answers → big attack animation → enemy flinches]

**Mito:** LET'S GO. see that? your brain is the weapon. kinda poetic.
[enemy does a small wind-up]
**Mito:** one more and it's done—

[Second card → answer → enemy defeated, win flourish]

**Mito:** gg. and that's… kinda the whole game. answer cards, win fights, get stronger.

---

## BEAT 2 — "what are you studying?" → generate decks
**Mito:** okay real question — what are you actually studying rn?

[Quick picker: chips like  Bio · Chem · MCAT · Vocab · Languages · Other  (+ optional free text). ONE step.]

**Mito:** say less. cooking up your decks…
[fast generate animation — a few cards flip into a deck]
**Mito:** boom. these are yours now. from here we fight with your real stuff.

---

## BEAT 3 — Battle → obtain Chloro + Neuro
[Next node. A lively green character appears.]

**Mito:** oh— company. that's Chloro. little menace, huge damage.
[short fight; answer 1–2 cards → win]
**Mito:** Chloro joined the squad 🎉 try not to let it go to her head.

[Next, a sturdy blue character blocks the path.]
**Mito:** and this tank is Neuro. basically nothing gets past him.
[fight → win]
**Mito:** Neuro obtained. okay, the squad's looking real now.

---

## BEAT 4 — Study mode (fast-forward + instant rewards)
**Mito:** now the part that actually makes you smarter — studying.
**Mito:** normally this is your real focus time. but first one's on me—
[fast-forward animation; rewards rain down: ATP, gold, XP]
**Mito:** see all that? ATP, gold, xp. you earn it by, y'know… actually studying. wild concept.

---

## BEAT 5 — Spend → level up + first ability → set the goal
**Mito:** let's spend it. pick someone to level up.
[player levels a character]
**Mito:** ooh — you just unlocked an ability. [Ability Name] hits way harder. save it for a boss.
**Mito:** and that's the loop: study → earn → level up → wreck bigger things.
**Mito:** endless mode, quizzes, your friends — that stuff opens up as you go. go play 👋
[Highlight next campaign node. Player is free.]

## Notes
- Keep BEATS 4–5 quick; the rest (ATP economy depth, endless, quiz/type mode, friends, PvP) drip in via later campaign nodes / level-gated unlocks — NOT here.
- Add a tiny "skip" for replayers, but default path should always pass through the aha (Beat 1).
- After Beat 1, optional one-tap: "👍 / 🤔" feedback so beta testers flag confusion.
- JP version: same beats, translate Mito's voice casual/cute (友達みたいなノリ) — ask for it when ready.
