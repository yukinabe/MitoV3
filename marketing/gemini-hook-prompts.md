# Mito — Hook Image Prompts (for Gemini / Nano Banana)

These generate the **lifestyle "hook" slides** (the relatable non-game opener). Game slides use real screenshots, never AI.

## How to use
1. Paste the **style prefix** + one scene below into Gemini/Nano Banana.
2. Ask for **4:5 vertical (1080×1350)** — matches the carousels. (1:1 works too.)
3. Generate 2–3 variations, pick the most candid/real one.
4. Send them back labeled by number (e.g. "here's #2 and #10") so I know where they go.

## Style prefix (prepend to every scene)
> Candid amateur iPhone photo, authentic and relatable, NOT a stock photo. Natural available light, slight grain, a little messy and real, shallow casual framing. No text, no captions, no watermarks, no readable brand logos or app names. Vertical 4:5 framing, leave calmer/simpler space toward the bottom for a text overlay. Subject not posing for the camera.

## Pro tips for the most "real" look
- Keep faces **candid / not looking at camera** — posed = stock = scroll-past.
- Ask for **imperfection**: clutter, mess, real dorm/library, mixed lighting.
- **No text or logos** in the image — I overlay the headline in Figma.
- Generate a couple variations; the slightly "worse"/messier one usually performs better.
- If you want a specific person/demographic, just add it (e.g., "a Black male student", "a girl with glasses") — variety across posts helps relatability.
