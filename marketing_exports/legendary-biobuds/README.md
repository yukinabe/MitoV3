# Legendary BioBud Concepts

Generated for UGC testing and future Mito character direction.

## Main Pair

Current preferred base assets:

- `legendary-biobuds-clean-base-sheet.png`
- `legendary-prion-biobud-clean-base.png`
- `legendary-t4-phage-biobud-clean-base.png`

Legendary treatment direction:

- Slightly bigger than normal BioBuds.
- More expressive faces than common characters.
- Rare body color accents are okay if they are part of the character material.
- Aura, sparkles, ripple marks, glow pixels, attack VFX, and rarity effects should be separate overlay assets, not baked into the base sprite.
- Biology concept should feel powerful, rare, iconic, or "OP" in real life.

## Effect Tests

Generated effect previews live in `effects/`.

Two workflows were tested:

1. Baked 4-frame effect spritesheet: base sprite + overlay composited into each frame for easy preview or video export.
2. Overlay-only 4-frame spritesheet: effect frames with transparent center, meant to be layered above the clean base sprite at runtime or in editing software.

Preview files:

- `effects/prion-base-overlay-baked-preview.png`
- `effects/t4-phage-base-overlay-baked-preview.png`
- `effects/prion-legendary-effect-baked-preview.gif`
- `effects/t4-phage-legendary-effect-baked-preview.gif`

Production direction: keep clean character sprites neutral, then apply effects as runtime/editor overlays.

### Prion BioBud

- Rarity: Legendary
- Real concept: misfolded infectious protein that can cause other proteins to misfold
- Why legendary: scary-powerful in real biology despite being tiny and simple
- Visual read: folded lavender protein ribbon, jagged misfold seam, mysterious ripple marks
- Personality: mischievous, cute but dangerous
- UGC angle: "i pulled a legendary prion BioBud"

### T4 Phage BioBud

- Rarity: Legendary
- Real concept: bacteriophage that attaches to bacteria and injects genetic material
- Why legendary: iconic viral nanomachine silhouette; visually boss-tier in real life
- Visual read: geometric capsid head, glowing DNA, tail tube, tail-fiber legs
- Personality: tough, determined final-boss energy
- UGC angle: "this one looks like a final boss"
